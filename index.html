Privacy Policy

App: Chicago Safe Zone Alert Pro
Developer: devsandpros
Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

Chicago Safe Zone Alert Pro is a safety-awareness application for Android. It is designed to help drivers stay alert when approaching designated safety zones in Chicago — areas identified by the City of Chicago as requiring heightened driver awareness, including zones near schools, parks, pedestrian crossings, and other high-activity public areas.

We are fully committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains clearly what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what is never done with it.

2. Location Data

The app uses your device's GPS to determine your position while driving. This is the sole function of the location permission — the app compares your real-time position against a built-in database of safety zones to provide timely audio and visual alerts as you approach them.

Your location is processed entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to any server, never recorded or stored outside your device, and never shared with any third party under any circumstances. The app cannot identify, track, or profile you in any way.

3. Safety Zones Database

The safety zones database used by the app is bundled directly inside the application at the time of installation. The app does not connect to any external server or download any data during normal operation. No internet connection is required for the app's core alert functionality. The database is updated as needed through regular app updates via the Google Play Store.

4. No Account Required

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

5. In-App Purchase

The app offers a one-time purchase to unlock the full version. All payment transactions are handled entirely and securely by the Google Play Store. We do not collect, see, process, or store any payment or billing information.

6. No Advertising

The app contains no third-party advertising SDKs and does not display any advertisements. No advertising identifiers are accessed or used.

7. Diagnostic Data

The app does not include any analytics or tracking libraries. We do not collect or transmit usage data.

Google Play may automatically collect anonymised crash and performance diagnostics through Android Vitals — a platform-level service built into the Google Play Store. This data is aggregated and anonymised by Google and is used solely to identify stability issues and improve app quality. It is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and is not accessible to us in any individually identifiable form.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will take immediate steps to address it.

9. Your Rights

Because this app does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, or delete. All app preferences are stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published at this same URL with an updated date at the top of the page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
