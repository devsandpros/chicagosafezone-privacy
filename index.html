Privacy Policy

App: Chicago Safe Zone Alert Pro
Developer: devsandpros
Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

Chicago Safe Zone Alert Pro is a safety-awareness application for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what is never done with it.

2. Location Data

The app uses your device's GPS to determine your position while driving. This is the core function of the app — it compares your real-time location against a built-in database of safety camera locations in Chicago to alert you when you are approaching one.

Your location data is processed entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to any server, never stored outside your device, and never shared with any third party under any circumstances.

3. Camera Dataset

The safety camera database used by the app is bundled directly inside the application. The app does not download data from any external source during normal operation. No internet connection is required for the app's core alert functionality.

4. No Account Required

The app does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information such as your name or email address.

5. In-App Purchase

The app offers a one-time purchase to unlock the full version. All payment transactions are handled entirely by the Google Play Store. We do not collect, process, or store any payment information.

6. No Advertising

The app contains no third-party advertising SDKs and does not display ads.

7. No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not include any analytics, crash-reporting, or tracking libraries. No usage data is collected or transmitted.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be published at this same URL with an updated date at the top.
